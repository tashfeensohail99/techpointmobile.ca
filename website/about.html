About us

A neighbourhood tech store built on nearly two decades of experience

TechPoint Mobile Solutions brings big-store capability and small-shop care to everyday mobile needs in Mississauga.

Our story

Making mobile simple for the neighbourhood

TechPoint Mobile Solutions was created to close a simple gap: Canada is one of the most connected countries in the world, yet buying a phone, activating a SIM or fixing a small issue can still mean long waits and confusing contracts.

We bring those services together in one accessible storefront — devices, accessories, prepaid SIM and eSIM activation, and everyday technical support — delivered with patience and transparency for local residents, students, seniors and cost-conscious households.

Mission & vision

Why we’re here

Mission

To provide accessible, reliable and affordable mobile technology products and everyday digital services to local communities in Canada — through a convenient neighbourhood retail model that delivers personalised support while creating local employment.

Vision

To become a trusted neighbourhood technology brand in Canada — recognised for simplifying mobile connectivity, expanding access to essential digital services, and growing into a scalable retail network that contributes to community development.
