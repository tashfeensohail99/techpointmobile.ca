Commitment

Accessibility

TechPoint Mobile Solutions is committed to accessibility for all customers, in line with the AODA and WCAG 2.1 AA.

TechPoint Mobile Solutions Limited is committed to providing an accessible experience for all customers, both in-store and online, in accordance with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA).

Our website

This website is designed with accessibility in mind and aims to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA — including readable colour contrast, keyboard navigation, descriptive text and a responsive layout that works across devices.

In our store

Our team is happy to provide patient, one-on-one assistance with device setup, activation and everyday tech questions.

Feedback

If you experience any difficulty accessing our website or services, please tell us so we can help and improve. Contact info@techpointmobile.ca or call +1 (437) 559-2990.
