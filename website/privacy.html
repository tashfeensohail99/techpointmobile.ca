Privacy Policy

How TechPoint Mobile Solutions collects, uses and protects personal information in line with Canada’s PIPEDA.

Effective date: 2026. TechPoint Mobile Solutions Limited (“TechPoint”, “we”) respects your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly — such as your name, phone number and email when you contact us, purchase a product, or activate a SIM/eSIM. For activations we may process information required by the relevant carrier to set up your service.

How we use it

  • To provide products, services and activations you request
  • To respond to enquiries and provide support
  • To meet legal, tax and record-keeping obligations

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to deliver a service you request (for example, with a carrier to activate a plan) or where required by law.

Protection & retention

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect personal information and retain it only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

Your choices

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information by contacting us at info@techpointmobile.ca.
